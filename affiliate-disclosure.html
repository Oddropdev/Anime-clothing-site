How Anime.clothing may earn commissions from links to stores and products.

Short version.

Anime.clothing may earn a commission when you click a product or store link and make a purchase. This does not change the price you pay.

Affiliate links.

Some links on Anime.clothing may be affiliate links. If you click one of these links and buy something from a partner store, Anime.clothing may receive a commission or referral fee.

These commissions help support the site.

Editorial approach.

Anime.clothing aims to recommend products, stores and categories based on relevance, quality, trust and fit with the site.

Affiliate relationships may influence which stores are available to link, but they should not override basic quality and trust checks.

Official and inspired products.

Anime.clothing may link to official licensed merch, verified retailers, collaborations and anime inspired clothing brands.

The site aims to avoid fake official claims, copied character art, stolen fan art and unclear licensing.

Third party stores.

Purchases are completed on third party websites. Those stores control their own prices, shipping, returns, customer service, terms and privacy policies.

Always review the store’s own product details and policies before buying.

Sponsored links.

Paid outbound links may use tracking tags and may be marked in the code as sponsored links. Product pages may also include visible notices when appropriate.

Amazon and other programs.

Anime.clothing may participate in affiliate programs such as Amazon Associates, Awin, Impact, ShareASale or direct brand partner programs.

As an affiliate site, Anime.clothing may earn from qualifying purchases.
