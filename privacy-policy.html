Privacy Policy.

How Anime.clothing handles basic site data, email signups, analytics and affiliate links.

Overview.

Anime.clothing is a curated clothing and affiliate content site. This page explains what information may be collected when you use the site.

Last updated: 2026

Information we collect.

  • Information you submit, such as an email address through a signup form.
  • Basic technical data, such as browser type, device type, pages viewed and approximate location.
  • Affiliate click data when you click links to partner stores.

How we use it.

  • To operate and improve the site.
  • To send updates if you join a mailing list.
  • To understand which pages and links are useful.
  • To track affiliate commissions from qualifying purchases.

Cookies and analytics.

Anime.clothing may use cookies, analytics tools and affiliate tracking technologies. These help measure traffic, clicks and site performance.

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings.

Affiliate links.

Some links may lead to third party stores. If you click one of these links and make a purchase, Anime.clothing may earn a commission.

Third party stores have their own privacy policies, terms, cookies and checkout systems.

Email.

If you subscribe to updates, your email address may be used to send clothing drops, store updates and related content. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails when an email service is connected.

Data sharing.

Anime.clothing does not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers such as analytics tools, email platforms, hosting providers and affiliate networks.

Your choices.

  • You can avoid submitting your email address.
  • You can block or delete cookies in your browser.
  • You can contact us about privacy related requests.

Contact.

For privacy questions, use the contact page.
